introduction

Cybersecurity refers to the practices, technologies, and processes that are designed to protect computer systems,
networks, and sensitive data from unauthorized access, theft, damage, and other forms of malicious attacks.
With the increasing reliance on technology in both personal and professional lives, the risks of cyber threats have also grown exponentially.
Cybersecurity has become a critical aspect of modern life, as it plays a crucial role in safeguarding businesses,
governments, and individuals from the numerous cyber threats that exist today.
It encompasses a broad range of areas, including network security, information security, application security, and endpoint security, among others.
In this age of digital connectivity, the importance of cybersecurity cannot be overstated, as the stakes are high for individuals and organizations alike.
